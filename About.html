About Valkyrie48

Valkyrie48 is an esports team that has been a part of JKT48 since 2018.

Currently, Valkyrie48 has 5 members out of a total of 25 members of JKT48, and they are members who have passion, enthusiasm, and deep knowledge in the world of gaming and esports.

Valkyrie48 is present as a solid and entertaining esports team, and aims to become a symbol of esports in Indonesia and expand it to the whole of Asia to the whole world.

Valkyrie48 is ready to present interesting and entertaining content, and still present the charm of JKT48 in every performance of theirs.
